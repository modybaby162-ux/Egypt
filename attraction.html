Pyrimids

Pyramids are geometric structures with a polygonal base and triangular faces that converge at an apex. Historically, they are most famous as the monumental tombs built by ancient Egyptian pharaohs, with the pyramids of Giza being the most well-known examples.

Egyptian Museum

The term "Egyptian Museum" most commonly refers to the historical Museum of Egyptian Antiquities in Cairo and the new, larger Grand Egyptian Museum (GEM) located near the Giza Pyramids which is scheduled for its official opening on November 1, 2025.
